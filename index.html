Understanding Firewood Quantities

What's in our measurements?

  • Bundle: 0.75 cubic feet, approximately 5-6 pieces. Perfect for a single evening fire.
  • 1/2 Face Cord: 21.33 cubic feet (4ft x 4ft x 16"), approximately 110-120 pieces. Lasts average household 4-6 weeks with regular use.
  • Face Cord: 42.67 cubic feet (4ft x 8ft x 16"), approximately 220-240 pieces. Ideal for 2-3 months of regular burning.
  • IBC Tote: 35.3 cubic feet (1 cubic meter), approximately 180-200 pieces. Convenient bulk storage option.

Note: A face cord is 1/3 of a full cord. A full cord measures 4ft x 4ft x 8ft (128 cubic feet).

The Importance of Moisture Content

Why moisture matters:

  • Optimal Range: 15-20% moisture content for best burning
  • Our Standard: All wood kiln-dried to below 20%
  • Problems with Wet Wood:
    • Difficult to ignite
    • Produces excessive smoke
    • Creates dangerous creosote buildup
    • Wastes energy evaporating water
    • Provides less heat output

üí° Tip: Use a moisture meter to test your firewood. Never burn wood above 20% moisture content.

Benefits of Kiln-Dried Wood

Why kiln-dried is superior:

  • Instant Ready: No waiting or seasoning required
  • Clean Burning: Minimal smoke and particulates
  • Maximum Heat: More BTUs per pound than air-dried
  • Pest-Free: Heat treatment eliminates insects and larvae
  • Mold-Free: No fungus or mildew concerns
  • Indoor Storage Safe: Can be stored inside without worry
  • Consistent Quality: Uniform moisture throughout

Proper Firewood Storage

Keep your investment protected:

  • Elevation: Stack wood at least 4" off the ground using pallets or rails
  • Coverage: Cover the top only, leaving sides open for airflow
  • Location: Store in sunny, breezy area when possible
  • Spacing: Leave gaps between logs for air circulation
  • Indoor Storage: Our kiln-dried wood is safe for basement or garage storage

‚ö†Ô∏è Never stack wood against your home's exterior walls - maintain at least 3 feet clearance to prevent pest and moisture issues.

Sustainable Wood Sourcing

Our commitment to sustainability:

Every piece of wood we sell comes from local tree services in the Pittsburgh area. Rather than letting this valuable resource go to waste in landfills, we:

  • Partner with certified arborists and tree removal services
  • Recycle storm-damaged and hazard trees
  • Reduce landfill waste by thousands of tons annually
  • Support local businesses and reduce transportation emissions
  • Turn urban tree waste into warmth for local homes

üå≥ Every cord of recycled firewood saves approximately 1.5 tons of wood from landfills and prevents methane emissions from decomposition.

Wood Species Guide

Common hardwoods in our mix:

  • Oak: Excellent heat output, long burn time, minimal sparking
  • Maple: Good heat, pleasant aroma, easy to split
  • Cherry: Medium heat, wonderful smell, beautiful flames
  • Ash: Great heat, low moisture naturally, burns very clean
  • Hickory: Highest heat output, long burning, ideal for cold nights

Our mixed hardwood provides an optimal blend of heat output, burn time, and ease of use.
